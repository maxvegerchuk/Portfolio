Travel platform login & signup upgrade

UX Design UI Design Product Design User Research

Overview

Streamlined signup and login process for Travellizy, a travel platform, boosting user engagement with a seamless, intuitive experience.

Project Details

Travellizy is a platform for booking airline tickets, aiming to provide a seamless experience for travelers. The primary goal of this initiative was to increase user engagement and time spent on the platform by introducing new features, including a social network for travelers and a travel blog.

Challenges

The registration form had 9 required fields, causing 70% of users to drop off. It took over 2 minutes to complete, leading to a low conversion rate (2.5%) and a high bounce rate (60%). We also needed to collect extra details like phone number and country without making the form more complicated.

Solution

I simplified the registration form to just 3 fields: email, password, and name. Extra details like country and phone number were moved to the profile settings. To speed up onboarding, I added Google and Facebook login options. After testing, the form now takes around 30-40ish seconds to complete.

Process

Results

The redesign significantly improved user engagement and conversion by simplifying the registration process and reducing friction.
