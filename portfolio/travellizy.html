Travellizy - Enhancing User Engagement Through Social Features

Travellizy is a platform for booking airline tickets, aiming to provide a seamless experience for travelers. The primary goal of this initiative was to increase user engagement and time spent on the platform by introducing new features, including a social network for travelers and a travel blog.

My Role

Product Designer

Timeline

2021-2022

Tools

Figma,
Adobe Photoshop,
Adobe Illustrator

Problem Statement


1. Low user engagement.
2. Short session durations.
3. Limited organic growth and insufficient user retention.
4. A need for more community-driven features to differentiate from competitors in the flight booking market.

Proposed Solutions

To address these challenges, I proposed three key solutions to increase user engagement and improve overall metrics:

Social Network for Travelers A dedicated space for users to share travel experiences, connect with other travelers, and discuss flight deals and destinations.

Travel Blog:A blog that features high-quality articles about destinations, travel tips, and flight deals to increase time spent on the platform.

Affiliate Program:Collaboration with travel bloggers and influencers to promote the platform and drive organic traffic through referral links.

Solution 1

Social Network for Travelers

Goal: Boost user engagement and interaction by creating a social community where travelers can connect, share travel experiences, and plan trips together.

Key Features of the Social Network
  • User profiles with travel history, preferences, and flight itineraries
  • Discussion boards and groups for specific destinations or types of travel
  • Photo sharing and tagging of destinations with recommendations for flights or deals
  • Ability to follow other users, comment, and like posts about travel experiences and flight recommendations

Club Page

Profile Page

User Albums

Expected Outcomes

30% increase in user engagement: Social features often increase daily active users by encouraging interaction and return visits.

20% increase in session duration: Social activities typically result in longer user sessions as users browse content and engage with others.

15% in user retention: Offering a personalized space for users to connect and share travel experiences encourages users to return to the platform to interact further.

Solution 3

Affiliate Program with Bloggers

Goal: Leverage travel influencers and bloggers to promote Travellizy, driving user growth through authentic recommendations and referrals.

Key Features
  • Partnerships with travel bloggers who share referral links to Travellizy's flight booking platform.
  • Influencers create content promoting Travellizy's deals and travel options, earning commission-based incentives.

Expected Outcomes

15% growth in new user registrations: Based on the success of affiliate marketing initiatives in the travel industry, this program is projected to drive user growth by increasing platform visibility through trusted influencers.

Conclusion

The proposed social network and blog aimed to address core user engagement challenges, while also creating a sense of community around flight bookings, driving long-term growth.

Although the social network was not implemented, the design was completed with significant projected impact based on industry benchmarks. The blog and affiliate program, once implemented, successfully increased user engagement, session duration, and organic traffic, establishing a solid foundation for future feature development.
